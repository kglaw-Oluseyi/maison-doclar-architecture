# Human Knowledge Assets Register
The founder is the highest-replacement-cost repository in the company. This register is a standing MKC obligation, not a one-time interview. Three kinds (ICAA ruling):

## Declarative — can be written
Protocol rules, VIP precedence, titles, address forms, invitation custom. → Elicited directly into MPS, marked `Founder-authored` until `Founder-verified`, ratified by constitutional process.

## Procedural — demonstrated by doing
Running check-in, managing live seating changes, recovering from event-day failure, vendor coordination under pressure. → Captured by observation: Event Intelligence lesson-capture during real events; runbooks written from observed practice, confidence `Observed` → `Validated`.

## Judgement — cannot be encoded as rules
"Should this governor outrank that chief at this wedding?" · "Should protocol bend here?" · "Is this vendor trustworthy despite yesterday?" → Preserved as **Decision Intelligence examples**: Situation · Decision · Reasoning · Alternatives · Outcome. These examples are the training corpus for future intelligence and the closest achievable preservation of founder intuition.

## Current asset list
Protocol judgement · host psychology · political etiquette · VIP conflict handling · room-reading heuristics · luxury service instincts · crisis behaviour · vendor trust memory · household memory.
