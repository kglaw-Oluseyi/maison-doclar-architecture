# Maison Knowledge Constitution (MKC)
**Status: v0.1 — outline ratified (Deliverable 05 §6, as amended). Full charter to follow.**

MKC is a constitutional system, not documentation: **every knowledge artifact is invalid unless MKC accepts it.** It is Git for knowledge.

## Articles (outline)
1. **Provenance** — every fact names its source, capture context, and the MPS/MXS versions in force.
2. **Confidence** — scored, never binary: `Canonical (constitutionally ratified)` · `Validated` · `Observed` · `Hypothesis` · `Historical`. Contradictions coexist with weights; newer does not mean truer; source trust is weighted.
3. **Authorship & approval** — who may create, who may verify; AI-generated content is marked distinctly from human-verified until reviewed. Founder content: `Founder-authored` → `Founder-verified` → ratified.
4. **Sensitivity** — field-level classes; special-category material (affiliations, risk, health/accessibility) under narrowest access with legal basis recorded. Obligations verified against current NDPA/GDPR advice; never assumed from this document.
5. **Lifecycle** — expiration, retention, deletion, legal hold.
6. **Human override** — always available, always recorded; an override is itself a Decision Intelligence fact.
7. **Negative knowledge** — failures are preserved with the same rigor as successes (`/knowledge/negative-knowledge.md`).
8. **Audit** — every read of sensitive knowledge is loggable.

MKC is versioned; pipelines and advisory services declare the MKC version they comply with.
