# Maison Experience System (MXS)
**Status: v0.1 — charter. Constitution to be authored by the group design authority.**

MXS is the operating philosophy of the Maison experience across every physical and digital interaction: typography, colour, motion, photography and video grammar, sound and voice, copy tone, print and physical standards (invitation cards, QR cards, badges, staff presentation, stage displays), interaction feel per audience register (Operator, Host, Planner, Guest, Event-day Staff), and brand behaviour under stress — how luxury behaves in error states, queues, and silence. Even loading states have a feeling.

Seed sources: `maison-doclar-shared/packages/ui/src/tokens.css` (design tokens), the badges design-workflow pattern, the established black/ivory/gold + Cormorant Garamond/Instrument Sans language, and the Frontend Experience & Surface Specification (five audience registers, component contracts, explainability display standards) from the Event OS specification corpus.

Every surface consumes MXS; only the design authority changes it. Products pin MXS versions.
