# Maison Doclar — Capability Catalogue
**Maison owns capabilities; systems realize them.** Capabilities are peers; none owns another. Full definitions: Deliverable 03 (as amended by Deliverables 04–05 ratifications).

## Tier 1 — Distinctive (the moat)
- **C1 · Maison Protocol System (MPS)** — constitutional asset, `/constitution/MPS`
- **C2 · Guest Intelligence** — Guest Knowledge Graph (Intelligence Plane) + operational guest data (Execution Plane)
- **C3 · Seating Intelligence** — frozen mathematical formulation is normative; every placement explainable
- **C4 · Maison Experience System (MXS)** — constitutional asset, `/constitution/MXS`
- **C5 · Event-Day Orchestration** — distributed truth, offline-first, lesson-capture obligation

## Tier 2 — Core
- **C6 · Communications** — Protocol Messaging layer (never sold) over Channel Engine (potentially commercial, stripped)
- **C7 · Guest Experience Delivery** — the upsell menu of the house
- **C8 · Media Production** — Studios; interfaces with Events via commissions only
- **C9 · Access & Guest Identity** — event-scoped; credentials die with the event; biometrics deletable by design
- **C10 · Reporting & Host Intelligence** — reports are MXS artifacts

## Tier 3 — Supporting
- **C11 · Revenue Operations** · **C12 · Vendor & Compliance Management** · **C13 · Planning & Scheduling** — realized by Nexus, permanently internal
- **C14 · Corporate & Knowledge Intelligence** — Orion-centred
- **C15 · Venue Intelligence** — Intelligence Plane graph, fed by C22
- **C16 · AI Governance** — policy, registries, budgets, routing, evaluation, observability; never in the execution path
- **C22 · Event Intelligence** — every event teaches Maison; capture is a workflow obligation
- **C23 · Vendor Intelligence** — knowledge, not records; Nexus owns facts and money, the Platform owns knowledge
- **C24 · Decision Intelligence** — situations, decisions, reasoning, alternatives, outcomes; seeded by `/decisions`

## Tier 4 — Commodity (never differentiate, never build twice)
- **C17 · Staff/Host/Planner Identity** · **C18 · Storage & Media Infra** · **C19 · Payments** · **C20 · Delivery Plumbing** · **C21 · Telemetry**

## Reserved
- **Maison Ontology System** — governs meaning; registered as future sibling (2–3 year horizon), not a current work item.
