# Executive Intelligence Vision (ARCH-004)
Maison Doclar is no longer being built primarily as an Event Operating System. It is being built as an **Autonomous Enterprise Operating System**, with luxury event operations as its **first realized domain**. The long-term objective is an **Executive Intelligence** capable of operating the enterprise under constitutional governance.

## The product, named
The product is the Executive Intelligence. The Platform is its body. The Intelligence Plane is its memory. The constitutional repository is its constitution. The capability modules are its skills. The repositories are its nervous system. The agents are its hands. **The human founder remains the CEO.** **The Executive Intelligence is not the enterprise; it is the constitutional operator of the enterprise** (DI-048): the enterprise remains founder-led, human-owned, constitution-governed. Scope: bounded permanently to the Maison Doclar estate; Urbanac remains excluded under DI-001; any other enterprise adopting this constitutional architecture would be a different programme, not an expansion of this one.

## Eventual abilities (each entering only through the constitutional process — DI-035)
Subject to constitutional authority: plan and execute long-horizon objectives; coordinate multiple specialist agents; monitor execution continuously; communicate with guests, vendors, and staff; generate creative assets; maintain documentation; analyse operational performance; modify and improve software; create pull requests; review implementations; deploy approved changes; challenge conflicting instructions; explain every significant action; learn continuously from outcomes. Abilities touching software change (modify/PR/review/deploy) constitute the reserved **Engineering Intelligence family** — a specialization of the Executive Intelligence, never a parallel system, inheriting all constitutional governance (DI-048).

## The constitutional principle this vision adds
**Capability is not authority.** The Executive Intelligence may possess technical capability without possessing constitutional authority. Possessing a PAT, deployment access, or email capability does not imply authority to use them. Authority derives exclusively from constitutional governance — MPS, MKC, AI Governance, CON-001/STD-008 as the sole intelligence interface, Decision Intelligence recording rationale, AMM evidence gating delegation, and human approvals wherever governance specifies. GOV-006's permanent human floors and AMM-001's evidence-earned promotions apply to the Executive Intelligence exactly as to any capability.

## Relationship to prior visions
ARCH-002 (Autonomous Event OS) is neither discarded nor rewritten: it becomes the **events-domain realization vision**, the first domain the Executive Intelligence operates. Every foundation built to date — two planes, advisory contracts, MKC, MPS, Decision Intelligence, capabilities, autonomy maturity — is precisely the substrate an enterprise-grade autonomous intelligence requires. What changes is the declared destination, so that implementation begins aimed at it.
